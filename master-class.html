Master Class

Master Class Program will be held in Aug. 13 - Aug. 14, 2016. Program includes:

Group class: Students sign up to perform and be coached by our distinguished faculties.

Workshop: Presenters will give practical tips and hands on activities for aspiring young musicians on music related topics.

Lecture: rofessional musicians of national and international acclaim will present lectures on music and music performance on music related topics.

Faculty concert: distinguished faculty from the 2016 USIMC Festival will perform.

Winners' Recital: the recital will be presented by the 2016 USIMC winners of all instrumental categories.
