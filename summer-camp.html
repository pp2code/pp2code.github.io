2016 Summer Camp Music Festival

Join us at this year’s Summer Camp. You will make a difference.

 
Free

Description

The best way in which children can spend their summers is by joining summer camps. They can learn a lot of things about different culture and make new friends with others. The camps help to develop the overall personality of a child.

This summer we offer a music camp. learn and perform with young musicians from all over the world, lead by a distinguished faculty and renowned conductors. During your time here, you will grow as a musician and as an artist, as you participate in and enjoy a summer of memorable performances.

Accordion

Day 1:
FlightFlight to San Francisco

We are on our way from Beijing to San Francisco, a 5,900 miles(9504 kilometers) journey to USA. Estimated flight time is 11 h 41 min. Time difference between San Francisco and Beijing is 15 Hours.

Day 2:
Tour in Braun Music Center

Braun Music Center is home to the Stanford Music Department. The facility houses faculty offices, classrooms, practice rooms, as well as Braun Rehearsal Hall, and Campbell Recital Hall. We will get familiar with the Rules of Competition in the afternoon.

Day 3:
CompetitionPaticipate in Music Competition

Competition includes following categories: Piano, violin, viola, cello, Traditional Chinese instruments categories are held annually. Guitar, harp and vocal are also included in the 2016 competition categories. All participants will receive a certificate of participation.

Day 4:
ClassGo to Master Class 1

Master class is a group class, students sign up to perform and be coached by our distinguished faculties. There are also lectures. Professional musicians of national and international acclaim will present lectures on music and music performance.

Day 5:
ClassGo to Master Class 2

The second day consists of workshops. Presenters will give practical tips and hands on activities for aspiring young musicians on music related topics. Winners' Recital will be in the end. The recital will be presented by the 2016 USIMC winners of all instrumental categories.

Day 6:
Tour in Stanford University

Stanford University is in the heart of Northern California’s dynamic "Silicon Valley,” home to Yahoo!, Google, Hewlett-Packard, and many other cutting-edge companies that were started by and continue to be led by Stanford alumni and faculty.The Stanford campus offers a wealth of sightseeing and educational opportunities. The Campus Walking Tour takes about 70 minutes.

Day 7:
Tour in Silicon Valley

It would be nice to take a trip down to Silicon Valley and visit/see the technology giants like Facebook, Google, Apple etc in order to give the kids some inspiration for studying hard at school.The Computer History Museum has lots of computers from ages gone by until now. It is quite entertaining and is quite nearby Intel.

Day 8:
Tour in San Francisco

San Francisco is home to a little bit of everything. Whether you’re a first time visitor or a long-time local. This is the place to find out about all things San Francisco. We will visit Golden Gate Bridge, Fisherman’s Wharf, Lombard Street, Union Square. If you’re lucky, you might see the California Sea Lions in Pier 39. We can also go to visit San Francisco Palace of Fine Art if time is enough.

Day 9:
FlightFlight to Beijing

After one week abroad, it’s time to say goodbye to your new friends and return home, bringing back new skills and unforgettable memories! You'll have free time today to stock up on some souvenirs to take home.

Day 10:
Back to Hometown

Back in Beijing again! Those who is not living in Beijing should buy another ticket to their hometown. After all those sightseeing, you’ll want a day to relax and take a rest. Have a good weekend with your family.
